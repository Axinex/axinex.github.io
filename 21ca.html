21st Century Arsenal

21st Century Arsenal, or 21CA is a Work-In-Progress modification for the game RimWorld by Ludeon Studios. 21CA is meant to fill a gap in the modded weapons scene: the 21st century. While there are a plethors of mods that add a weapon or two here and there, there aren't really any mods that add a wide selection of balanced, lore-friendly weapons from the current century (looking at you, Project Armory!). This mod intends to remedy this gap in RimWorld's modification community.
